BIOGRAPHY

A little about Christy Fong:

Background

Born in South Korea and raised in Fremont CA, I was adopted into a Chinese American family along with my adopted brother. I am now 22 years old attending SFSU majoring in Visual Communication Design and minoring in Education.

Why Design?

I like Art and being creative so I didn't mind further branching out my skills to learn design as well.

Hobbies

My hobbies include; drawing, listening to music, baking, skating, watching Studio Ghibli movies, and watching game plays. But by far my favorite is hanging out with my friends and just having a good time together.
